## How to use these videos

1. **Foundation — John Savill''s Foundry / AI-agents deep dives**. Pick the most recent ones. Savill''s whiteboard explanations are the fastest way to internalize how Foundry resources, projects, agents, tools, and tracing fit together.
2. **First-party demos — Microsoft Reactor and Microsoft Build sessions**. These are the canonical Microsoft demos for Foundry Agent Service, evaluation, content safety, MCP tool integration, and prompt orchestration. They use the exact terminology and patterns the modern question pool expects.
3. **Service walkthroughs — AI Show**. Use it for service-specific gaps: AI Search hybrid + semantic ranker, Document Intelligence, Speech, content safety.

> When an official AI-103 course is published by Microsoft, this page will be updated to pin it as the primary entry.
